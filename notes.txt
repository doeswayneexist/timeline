First steps

what does it do?

Creates a time line.

Begin with an empty line.

User enters the first event.

Puts it in the middle of the time line.

----------------V----------------

User enters the second event.

Determine where on the timeline it goes.

---------v-----------v-----------

Always put the two furthest points at the same place on the timeline.

--v---------------------------v--

User keeps entering events and the events are placed accordingly on the timeline.

keep track of both extremes.
